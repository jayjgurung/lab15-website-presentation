Profile

In my professional journey spanning over 5 years, I've immersed myself in the realms of audio engineering, music production, and artistic collaboration. This experience has allowed me to develop a nuanced understanding of the intricacies within these domains. Along the way, I've had the privilege of collaborating with over 200 talented artists, each interaction contributing to my growth and proficiency. Specifically, I've focused on mastering the tools and techniques within digital audio workstations, refining my skills to ensure a seamless and polished production process. Through these collaborations, I've not only honed my technical expertise but also gained valuable insights into the diverse and dynamic world of music creation. My commitment to continuous learning and collaboration remains at the forefront, as I strive to contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving landscape of audio and music.



Education & Skills

My passion for My fascination with technology originated during my middle school years, where I delved into programming languages such as Q-Basic, C++, JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS. Subsequently, I pursued formal education in music theory, vocal performance, and guitar performance under the guidance of seasoned professionals at a distinguished institution in NMC, Kathmandu. Furthering my academic journey, I engaged in a diverse array of STEM courses during my bachelor's studies, complemented by a minor in music production. Currently, at CityTech, I am dedicated to refining my expertise in the intersection of technology and the arts. Specifically, I am pursuing a degree in Emerging Media Technology with a focused concentration in music, aligning my academic pursuits with my passion for both technology and musical expression.



Projects & Ambitions

Presently, I am combining my proficiency in music, coding, art, and technology to forge innovative products aimed at fostering creativity. My overarching objective is to harness my coding expertise and grasp of physical computing to craft tools that not only elevate the creative processes for artists but also resonate with individuals seeking to integrate technology into their daily lives for enrichment. In essence, my focus extends beyond the realm of artistic collaboration to cater to a broader audience, encompassing everyday enthusiasts who find value in using technology as a means of enhancing various facets of their lives. By marrying my multifaceted skill set, I aspire to contribute to the creation of tools and experiences that seamlessly blend creativity, technology, and accessibility, thereby making innovative solutions available to a diverse spectrum of users.
