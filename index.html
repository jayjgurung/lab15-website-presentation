Paper : "The adapation of technology has enhanced the way we experience art and culture"

- Jay J Gurung
Read my take on how technology enhances the audience's experience of art and culture.

The article discusses the transformative role of technology in art and culture, emphasizing its capacity to enhance audience experiences and provide new dimensions for engagement. It highlights the innovative projects of artists such as Drake-Brockman and Miral Kotb, who integrate cutting-edge technology into their works. Drake-Brockman's "Floribots" combines origami and robotics, resulting in unexpected emergent phenomena, while his other projects involve audience interaction through technology. Miral Kotb, on the other hand, infuses illumination technology into dance, creating the "ILuminate" project, where performers wear outfits with lighting technology to synchronize and illustrate unseen imageries. Both artists share a focus on audience participation, the critical role of technology, and leveraging existing elements of human culture to add new dimensions. The article concludes that technology in art and culture elevates experiences, promotes creativity, and necessitates skills in coding, electronics fabrication, and physical computation for a deeper understanding.

Paper : "Unveiling the Essence of Humanity: The Inherent Capacity for Continuous Improvement”

- Jay J Gurung
Is our human desire to improve what makes us human unique? Read here

At the heart of the narrative lies a profound question that has intrigued scholars throughout history: what defines the essence of being human? Diving into this inquiry from a diverse array of perspectives, the paper posits a compelling thesis — "the innate nature of human beings to improve themselves is what makes them human." Meticulously curated from a rich tapestry of resources and articles, this research delves into the distinctive trait that sets humans apart: the relentless drive to enhance themselves. As you traverse through the pages, discover how humans, amidst sharing the same earthly resources as myriad other living beings, stand uniquely characterized by their unparalleled ability to refine and elevate their capabilities. From the dawn of civilization to the present day, witness the unyielding progression in technology, innovation, art, and culture — an evolution that defines the very essence of the thriving human species. Join me in unraveling the fascinating journey of self-improvement that epitomizes what it truly means to be human.
